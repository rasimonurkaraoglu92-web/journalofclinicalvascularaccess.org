About

A focused forum for vascular access science

JCVA publishes original research, reviews, technical notes, and case reports covering all aspects of vascular access — from bedside peripheral insertion to long-term central venous device management. Our scope bridges critical care, surgery, oncology, nephrology, and interventional practice.


We are committed to open-access dissemination, rigorous peer review, and rapid editorial decisions to serve clinicians and researchers worldwide.

Aims & Scope

Central Venous Access

CVC insertion techniques, tunneled catheters, implantable ports, PICC lines, and hemodialysis access management.

Ultrasound Guidance

Real-time sonographic guidance for vascular procedures, sonoanatomy, needle visualization, and point-of-care applications.

Peripheral Vascular Access

Difficult venous access, midline catheters, pediatric and neonatal access, arterial lines, and IO access techniques.

Complications & Safety

Catheter-related infections, thrombosis, malposition, mechanical complications, and quality improvement initiatives.

Education & Simulation

Vascular access training programs, simulation-based education, competency assessment, and curriculum development.

Device Innovation

Novel catheter technologies, insertion devices, tip confirmation systems, securement, and biocompatibility research.

Ethics & Submission

Publication Ethics

JCVA adheres to COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines. All submissions must include:

  • Ethics committee approval (if applicable)
  • Informed consent statement
  • Conflict of interest declaration
  • Author contribution statement (CRediT)
  • Data availability statement

How to Submit

Manuscripts are currently accepted by email while our online system is under development.

Send your manuscript, title page, cover letter, and supplementary files to:

editor@journalofclinicalvascularaccess.org

We aim to provide an initial editorial decision within 14 days of submission.
